blood-O-nation

blood-O-nation is an organisation that aims at connecting generous blood donors with recipients in need throughout India. It is one of the fastest responding websites. We work 24/7 keeping in mind that life waits for none and it is blood that runs life. This website is run free of cost. All these reasons make our website one of the most efficient in our field of work.

Our branches:

We have our branches at Banglore, Mumbai, Delhi and Kolkata. There is a huge network of volunteers connecting different parts of the country to these main branches. This helps us in reaching out to people in need at the earliest possible.

Method of operation:

We do not operate like a blood bank. We connect a recipient with compatible donors. Getting in touch with the donor and working it out is left to the recipient.

What do we gain from this?

This is an act of humanity. We expect no money or any other form of repay. In simple, this service is available for free of cost.
